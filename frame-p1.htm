Design Principles for HCI

An article from Brian Ambielli which provides an overview of 15 principles for human centered design. The author goes on to define all of the principles, which I will avoid doing here for brevity. The most important take away is that consistency trumps originality.

https://bambielli.com/posts/2018-08-06-fifteen-principles-for-human-centered-design/

An article from Ben Scneirderman who discusses his "Eight Golden Rules of Interface Design". The rule that comes in at No.1 is "Strive for consistency."

https://www.designprinciplesftw.com/collections/shneidermans-eight-golden-rules-of-interface-design

Both authors have consistency at the top of both of their lists. Simplicity is alo very important in both of the articles and there is quite a bit of overlap.
