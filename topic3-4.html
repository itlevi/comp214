3.4 Evaluate a VR Interface – Google Earth

I really enjoyed using Google Earth and I am pretty excited to see that it runs in the browser instead of having to download an application.

The app is incredibly easy to use and very intuitive. Having very little prior experience I was able to move around the world and navigate through the different options.

I did notice some issues with my high-end MacBook Pro, primarily with globe animations being choppy. I didn’t do a deep dive into exactly how much resources the app was consuming, but I did notice that it was consuming a lot of battery quite quickly.

The only glitches I encountered during my use of the app were some missing polygons on the sides of some buildings. While not a bug, I was a little annoyed of being prompted to log in to access some of the more advanced features like Projects.
