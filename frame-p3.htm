Specialized Output Devices and Their Applications

A 3D printer is a specialized output device. 3D printers take a digital model and output a physical object. 3D printing is quite frequently used in rapid prototyping in the manufacturing design process. It also has incrasing usage in medicine and even in the food manufacturing process.

3D projectors are similar to regular projects as they both output light to display an image on a 2D surface. The 3D projecter differs by using specialized glasses to add depth to the image. They are used primarily in movie theatres and homes to increase immesion in movies and video games.
